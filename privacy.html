Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026
We believe in plain English. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. No legal jargon — just honesty.

1. Who we are

VoiceMyResume is an AI-powered resume building service available at voicemyresume.com. We help everyday workers create professional resumes quickly and easily.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: support@voicemyresume.com

2. What information we collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide our service:

  • Email address — if you sign up for updates or create an account
  • Resume content — the work experience and information you enter to build your resume
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use our site (pages visited, features used) to help us improve the service

We do NOT collect your Social Security number, government ID, or any sensitive personal identification.

3. How we use your information

We use your information only to:

  • Build and improve your resume using AI
  • Process your Pro subscription payment through Stripe
  • Send you important account and service updates
  • Improve our service based on how people use it
  • Respond to your support requests

We do NOT sell your information to anyone. Ever. We do NOT use your information for advertising. We do NOT share your resume content with third parties.

4. AI processing of your resume content

When you build a resume, your work experience is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate professional resume text. This is the core of our service. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

We recommend not including highly sensitive personal information (like Social Security numbers or passwords) in your resume content.

5. Payments and Stripe

All payments for VoiceMyResume Pro ($4.99/month) are processed by Stripe, a trusted and secure payment processor. When you subscribe:

  • Your payment details go directly to Stripe — we never see your card number
  • Stripe stores your payment information securely
  • You will receive receipts directly from Stripe by email
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through Stripe's customer portal

Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

6. Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Your Claude API key (so you don't have to enter it every time)
  • Your email address if you sign up for updates

This data stays on your device only — it is never sent to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

7. Data retention

We keep your account information for as long as you have an active account. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days. Resume content processed through our AI is not permanently stored on our servers.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Cancel your Pro subscription at any time
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@voicemyresume.com

9. Children's privacy

VoiceMyResume is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of VoiceMyResume after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

Questions or concerns about your privacy? We're here to help.

Email: support@voicemyresume.com
Website: voicemyresume.com
